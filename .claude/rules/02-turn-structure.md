# 5. Game Structure

> Source: Star Wars Unlimited Comprehensive Rules v7.0 (3/6/26)

---

## 5.1 General

- 5.1.1 A game consists of multiple rounds; each round has an action phase and a regroup phase.
- 5.1.2 Each step of setup and each phase step must be completed in full unless otherwise specified.
- 5.1.3 Before starting, each player needs a deck (with leader and base), token cards their deck creates, damage counters, and one initiative counter.

---

## 5.2 Starting the Game and Setup

Steps in order:

**a. Put bases into play.** Each player places their base at the top-center of their own play area.

**b. Put leaders into play.** Each player places their leader below their base, Leader side faceup. (If a leader has multiple Leader sides, it indicates which begins faceup.)

**c. Determine the first player.** Randomly choose a player. That player decides who begins with the initiative counter (they may give it to an opponent). Set all other counters and tokens aside out-of-play.

**d. Shuffle decks and draw opening hands.** Each player shuffles their deck, then draws 6 cards.

**e. Choose whether to take a mulligan.** Each player may shuffle their entire hand into their deck and draw a new hand of 6. Only one mulligan; must keep the new hand. The player with the initiative decides first, then their opponent.

**f. Resource two cards.** Each player chooses 2 cards from their hand and puts them into play as resources, facedown and ready. Must place exactly two.

Then, start the first round with the first action phase.

---

## 5.3 Round

- 5.3.1 A round = an action phase followed by a regroup phase. The start of each action phase is also the start of a new game round.
- 5.3.2 An ability that refers to "this round" encompasses both the action phase and regroup phase of that round.

---

## 5.4 Action Phase

- 5.4.1 The action phase consists of: **Start of phase step → Player turns → End of phase step.**
  - **a. Start of action phase.** Lasting effects that expire at the start of the action phase/round expire now. Abilities that trigger at the start of the action phase/round trigger now.
  - **b. Player turns.** Players alternate taking one action at a time. The player with the initiative goes first. After their action and any triggered abilities resolve, the opponent becomes active. Continue until both players pass consecutively. Five possible actions: Play a Card, Attack With a Unit, Use an Action Ability, Take the Initiative, or Pass. See 1.15.
  - **c. End of action phase.** Lasting effects that expire at the end of the action phase (e.g., "for this phase") expire now.
- 5.4.2 After the action phase, proceed to the regroup phase. Any triggered abilities waiting to resolve must resolve before beginning the regroup phase.

---

## 5.5 Regroup Phase

- 5.5.1 The regroup phase has five steps in order:
  - **a. Start of regroup phase.** Lasting effects that expire at the start of the regroup phase expire now. Abilities that trigger at the start of the regroup phase trigger now.
  - **b. Draw cards.** Each player draws 2 cards.
  - **c. Resource cards.** Starting with the active player, each player may choose 1 card from their hand to resource, facedown and exhausted. Players may choose not to.
  - **d. Ready cards.** Each player readies all exhausted cards they control: units, resources, and their leader.
  - **e. End of regroup phase.** Lasting effects that expire at the end of the regroup phase or round (e.g., "for this phase," "for this round") expire now.
- 5.5.2 After the regroup phase ends, a new round begins with a new action phase.

---

## 5.6 Ending the Game

- 5.6.1 A game ends immediately once a player's base reaches 0 remaining HP and is defeated. That player loses; their opponent wins.
- 5.6.2 Once a player's base has 0 remaining HP, they cannot take any actions or resolve any abilities or effects.
- 5.6.3 If both players' bases reach 0 simultaneously, the game ends in a draw.
- 5.6.4 A player can concede at any time (e.g., by saying "I concede"). They lose immediately.
