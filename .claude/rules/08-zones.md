# 4. Zones

> Source: Star Wars Unlimited Comprehensive Rules v7.0 (3/6/26)

---

## 4.1 General

- 4.1.1 Seven zone types: each player's **base zone**, the shared **ground arena**, the shared **space arena**, each player's **resource zone**, each player's **deck**, each player's **hand**, and each player's **discard pile.** Ground and space arenas are shared; all others are separate per player.
- 4.1.2 Cards that are **set aside** are not in any zone.
- 4.1.3 The "game area" = all zones collectively.

---

## 4.2 Base Zone

- 4.2.1 Each player has their own base zone in the center of their play area. Each player's base and leader start here.
- 4.2.2 Bases always remain in their owner's base zone. Leaders move from the base zone to an arena when deployed, and return to the base zone when defeated.
- 4.2.3 Units in either arena may attack enemy bases directly without leaving their arena.
- 4.2.4 A player's Force token is created in their base zone. See 8.38 / 09-special-rules.md.

---

## 4.3 Ground Arena

- 4.3.1 The ground arena is a shared zone next to each player's base zone. Ground units are played here and face their controller when ready.
- 4.3.2 Arena sides are established when the first unit of the game is played — its arena type determines which side of the base zone is ground and which is space. These sides remain fixed for the entire game.
- 4.3.3 Friendly ground units can attack enemy ground units and the enemy base. They do not leave the arena when attacking the enemy base.
- 4.3.4 Ground units **cannot** attack enemy units in the space arena unless an ability specifically allows it. However, ground units may deal damage to space arena units through abilities.
- 4.3.5 Units in the ground arena are "ground units" regardless of their printed arena type.

---

## 4.4 Space Arena

- 4.4.1 The space arena is a shared zone next to each player's base zone. Space units are played here and face their controller when ready.
- 4.4.2 Arena sides established the same way as ground (see 4.3.2). The side is mirrored from each player's perspective.
- 4.4.3 Friendly space units can attack enemy space units and the enemy base. They do not leave the arena when attacking the enemy base.
- 4.4.4 Space units **cannot** attack enemy units in the ground arena unless an ability specifically allows it. However, space units may deal damage to ground arena units through abilities.
- 4.4.5 Units in the space arena are "space units" regardless of their printed arena type.

---

## 4.5 Resource Zone

- 4.5.1 Cards "resourced" are placed in the resource zone as "resources" and can be exhausted to pay costs. See 1.7.
- 4.5.2 Resources are placed facedown and remain facedown. A player may view their own at any time (opponent can't).
- 4.5.3 Players may add one card from their hand to the resource zone during each regroup phase.
- 4.5.4 Credit tokens are created in the resource zone but are NOT resources.

---

## 4.6 Deck

- 4.6.1 Each player's deck is its own zone. Cards are facedown, out-of-play, and can't be viewed except via abilities.
- 4.6.2 Cards leave the deck when drawn, discarded, or played. A card is NOT considered to leave the deck when searched, looked at, or revealed (unless immediately drawn, discarded, or played).

---

## 4.7 Hand

- 4.7.1 Each player's hand is its own zone. No limit to cards in hand.
- 4.7.2 Cards enter hand when drawn or when an ability returns them.
- 4.7.3 Cards leave hand when played or discarded. Not considered to leave when looked at or revealed (unless immediately played/discarded).
- 4.7.4 Cards in hand may only be viewed by their owner. The number of cards in hand is open information; the cards' identities are hidden from the opponent.

---

## 4.8 Discard Pile

- 4.8.1 Each player has their own discard pile. Played events, defeated (non-leader) units, defeated upgrades, and discarded cards go here, faceup.
- 4.8.2 Cards in the discard pile are open information; any player may view them at any time.
- 4.8.3 Order of cards in a discard pile does not need to be maintained; may be rearranged at any time.
- 4.8.4 If an ability allows playing a card from the discard pile, still pay all costs (including modifiers and additional costs). After an event is played from the discard pile, it re-enters as a new copy.

---

## 4.9 In-Play and Out-of-Play

- 4.9.1 **In-play zones**: each player's base zone, ground arena, space arena, and each player's resource zone. Faceup cards in these zones are "in play" and can affect the game through abilities, power, and HP. Facedown card sides are out-of-play.
- 4.9.2 **Out-of-play zones**: each player's deck, hand, and discard pile. Cards here are "out of play" by default and cannot affect the game unless an ability explicitly states they can affect an out-of-play zone or resolve from one.

---

## 4.10 Play Area

- 4.10.1 A player's "play area" = cards they control in in-play zones, **except** upgrades they control attached to enemy units (which are in the unit controller's play area). Includes: cards in base zone, cards in resource zone, units in arenas controlled by this player, upgrades attached to units they control.
- 4.10.2 If a player plays an upgrade onto an enemy unit, that player controls the upgrade but it is NOT in their play area.
- 4.10.3 No limit on the number of cards a player can control in their play area.

---

## 4.11 Set Aside / Being in No Zone

- 4.11.1 When a card is set aside, it is out-of-play and not in any zone.
- 4.11.2 Token cards begin the game set aside and are put into play by certain abilities. When a token leaves play, it is set aside again.
